FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

😝 Can CleanX wash delicate and traditional fabrics?

Absolutely. We handle lace, ankara, aso-oke, suits, and delicate fabrics using specialised methods that preserve texture, colour, and overall quality.

🌿 Are CleanX detergents safe for sensitive skin?

Yes. We use high-quality, skin-friendly detergents that are safe for children and sensitive skin while also reducing environmental impact.

🚚 Do you offer pickup and delivery?

Absolutely! We offer free pickup and delivery across Imo State. Simply call us or send a message and we’ll schedule a convenient time for you.

⏱ How long does a typical order take?

Standard turnaround is 24 to 48 hours. Express and same-day options are available depending on your location and selected service.

💳 What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfers, and major mobile payment platforms. Payment is conveniently made upon delivery.
