Our Services

Everything Your Laundry Needs

We offer a full range of garment care services designed to keep your clothes fresh, clean, and looking their best.

Wash & Fold Service

We wash, dry, and neatly fold your clothes for you. Saving time for busy customers who don’t want to handle laundry themselves. Clothes are returned clean and ready to wear.

Dry Cleaning

We use special cleaning solvents instead of water to clean delicate fabrics, maintaining quality, colour, and texture of sensitive garments. Best suited for suits and formal wear.

Ironing & Pressing

Removes wrinkles and gives clothes a sharp, polished look. Perfect for office wear, special outfits, and uniforms. Ensures your garments look professional and well-finished.

Stain Removal Treatment

Focuses on removing tough stains like oil, ink, or wine. Specialised products and techniques are used to protect the fabric while cleaning, helping to extend the life of your clothing.

Commercial Laundry

Handles large volumes for hotels, hospitals, restaurants, and gyms. Industrial machines ensure deep cleaning and hygiene, guaranteeing consistency, efficiency, and quick turnaround.

Book Now

Bedding & Linen Cleaning

Bedsheets, duvets, curtains, and pillowcases professionally cleaned. We remove dust, allergens, and odours effectively, maintaining a clean and healthy sleeping environment.

Garment Repair & Alteration

Fabric Care & Specialist Cleaning
