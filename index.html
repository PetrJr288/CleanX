FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

⏱️ How long does CleanX take to return my clothes?

Our standard turnaround time is 24 to 48 hours depending on your selected service. Express and same-day delivery options are available in selected locations.

🎨 Does CleanX separate clothes before washing?

Yes. Clothes are sorted by colour, fabric type, and care instructions to prevent colour bleeding, shrinkage, and damage.

🛡️ What if my clothes are damaged or misplaced?

CleanX follows strict quality control processes. In rare cases of damage or loss, customers should report immediately so the issue can be reviewed and resolved fairly.

🚚 Do you offer pickup and delivery?

Absolutely! We offer free pickup and delivery across Imo State. Simply call us or send a message and we'll schedule a convenient time for you.

💳 What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfers, and major mobile payment platforms. Payment is made upon delivery for your convenience.
