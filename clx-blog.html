Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers to common questions about CleanX Laundry Services.

⏱ How long does CleanX take to return my clothes?

Our standard turnaround time is 24 to 48 hours depending on your selected service. Express and same-day delivery options are available in selected locations across Imo State.

🎨 Does CleanX separate clothes before washing?

Yes. Clothes are sorted by colour, fabric type, and care instructions to prevent colour bleeding, shrinkage, and damage, ensuring your garments remain in excellent condition.

🛡 What if my clothes are damaged or misplaced?

CleanX follows strict quality control processes. In rare cases of damage or loss, customers should report immediately so the issue can be reviewed and resolved fairly.

😝 Can CleanX wash delicate and traditional fabrics?

Absolutely. We handle lace, ankara, aso-oke, suits, and delicate fabrics using specialised methods that preserve texture, colour, and overall quality.

🌿 Are CleanX detergents safe for sensitive skin?

Yes. We use high-quality, skin-friendly detergents that are safe for children and sensitive skin while also reducing environmental impact.

👕 Is ironing and folding included?

Ironing and folding depend on the selected package. Some plans include them automatically, while others allow customers to add these services affordably.

📦 How do I book a CleanX pickup?

Booking is easy via phone call, WhatsApp, or our website. CleanX offers flexible pickup and delivery schedules tailored to busy Nigerian lifestyles.
